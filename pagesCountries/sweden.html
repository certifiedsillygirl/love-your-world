ecotourism in sweden

aurora sky station - abisko

The Aurora Sky Station in Northern Sweden is one of the best places in the world to view Aurora Borealis - the Northern Lights. The lights become visible from September, but you may still enjoy the Sky Station at any other time of year, as it offers midnight sun hiking during summer and chairlift rides all year round.

sapmi nature - laponia

At Sapmi Nature, you will be the guest of Sweden's indigenous people - the Sami. They will teach you all about their culture, through aspects such as preparation and cooking of food, fishing, reindeer herding, excursions in nature and storytelling.

treehotel - harads

At the Treehotel, nature, ecological values, comfort, and design are combined to give a truly unique experience. There are many different kinds of rooms including the Biosphere, the Mirrorcube, the UFO, the Dragonfly, and many more. Many activities are also offered, from Western horse riding, dogsledding to ice fishing, forest SPA and ice dining or river hot tub, designed to match the time of year.
