ecotourism in france

calanches de piana and scandola reserve cruise - corsica

Discover a beautiful UNESCO world heritage sight - Calanches de Piana and its red rocks, the Scandola reserve and the small hamlet of Girolata, accessible only on foot or by boat

cen auvergne - auvergne

A conservation site for the natural spaces of Aurvergne. CEN Auvergne manages a network of 250 sites, i.e. 2,600 hectares. It also manages several conservatory orchards of old fruit varieties. It is managed by a not-for-profit organisation

pointe du raz cap sizun - brittany

Cap Sizun is home to many species of birds, including the famous European herring gull. Pointe du Raz has been preserving the wellbeing of the birds since 1996. You can find binoculars and telescopes at the entrance to observe the fauna.
