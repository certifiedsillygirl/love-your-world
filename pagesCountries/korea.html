ecotourism in south korea

pureun arboretum (푸른수목원) - seoul

An arboretum that preserves and protects a variety of plant species. It offers exhibitions and educational, eco-friendly activities such as gardening. It also includes a reading café, for those who are tired and wish to rest.

hallasan (한라산) - jeju island

Hallasan is the tallest mountain in South Korea. The area around it is a national park - the Hallasan National Park. It is a dormant volcano with a lake at its crater. It is most beautiful at springtime, which is when the azaelia flowers blossom.

changdeokgung palace (창덕궁) - seoul

Changdeokgung Palace; known as "The Palace of Prospering Virtue". It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and is regarded as a masterpiece of Korean palace architecture. Changdeokgung is one of the "Five Grand Palaces" built by the kings of the Joseon Dynasty, and was built by the third king of Joseon, King Taejong.
