ecotourism in thailand

the elephant nature park - chiang mai

The Elephant Nature Park is a leading rescue and rehabilitation center in Northern Thailand, founded by the renowned elephant rights advocate Saengduean Chailert, known as Lek. The park has rescued and cared for elephants who were subject to cruel practices such as street begging, elephant riding, and circus shows, from which they have suffered both physically and psychologically. In the park, they live freely in nature, and are treated with love and respect.

new heaven reef conservation program - koh tao

The New Heaven Reef Conservation Program is located on the Thai island of Koh Tao. The organisation conducts a variety of marine conservation practices, from maintenance of artificial coral reefs to caring for baby turtle hatchlings. The program also offers daily projects for people to participate in, regardless of experience.

bangkok treehouse - bangkok

This travel sustainable hotel offers stylish three-storey living units that are furnished with modern furniture and come with free Wi-Fi. Sightseeing tours are also available from the tour desk, and other requests may be made at the 24-hour front desk. A great variety of dishes made with organic ingredients is served at their Reflect Restaurant.
